About Me

I’ve always been interested in human behavior since I was a child. I love to learn about people and why they do what they do.


Experience and product design came as a natural progression for me, because my interests of design and people became users and their interactions with the things they use every day.


My goal is to create products and implement systems that make a positive difference in people’s lives.
